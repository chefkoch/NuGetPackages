mkvinfo
===

[mkvinfo] is part of [MKVToolNix].

Details
---
  - Installation command: ``Install-Package mkvinfo``

Content
---
  - Copies ``mkvinfo.exe`` during ``BeforeBuild``-targets to ``$(TargetDir)``
