mkvpropedit
===

[mkvpropedit] is part of [MKVToolNix].

Details
---
  - Current version: 6.3.0
  - Installation command: ``Install-Package mkvpropedit``

Content
---
  - Copies ``mkvpropedit.exe`` during ``BeforeBuild``-targets to ``$(TargetDir)``
