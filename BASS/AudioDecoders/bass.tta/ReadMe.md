bass.tta
===

[bass.tta] is an extension enabling the playback of TTA encoded files.

Details
---
  - Installation command: ``Install-Package bass.tta``

Content
---
  - Copies ``bass_tta.dll`` during ``BeforeBuild``-targets to ``$(TargetDir)``
