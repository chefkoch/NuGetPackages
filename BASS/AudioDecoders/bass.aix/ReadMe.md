bass.aix
===

[bass.aix] is an extension enabling the playback of AIX encoded files.

Details
---
  - Installation command: ``Install-Package bass.aix``

Content
---
  - Copies ``bass_aix.dll`` during ``BeforeBuild``-targets to ``$(TargetDir)``
