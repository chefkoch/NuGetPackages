bass.adx
===

[bass.adx] is an extension enabling the playback of ADX encoded files.

Details
---
  - Installation command: ``Install-Package bass.adx``

Content
---
  - Copies ``bass_adx.dll`` during ``BeforeBuild``-targets to ``$(TargetDir)``
