bass.ac3
===

[bass.ac3] is an extension enabling the playback of AC3 encoded files and streams.

Details
---
  - Installation command: ``Install-Package bass.ac3``

Content
---
  - Copies ``bass_ac3.dll`` during ``BeforeBuild``-targets to ``$(TargetDir)``
