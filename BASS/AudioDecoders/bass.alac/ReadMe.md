bass.alac
===

[bass.alac] is an extension enabling the playback of ALAC (Apple Lossless) encoded files.

Details
---
  - Installation command: ``Install-Package bass.alac``

Content
---
  - Copies ``bass_alac.dll`` during ``BeforeBuild``-targets to ``$(TargetDir)``
