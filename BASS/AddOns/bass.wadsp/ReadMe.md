bass.wadsp
===

[bass.wadsp] is an extension enabling the use of Winamp DSP plugins with BASS.

Details
---
  - Installation command: ``Install-Package bass.wadsp``

Content
---
  - Copies ``bass_wadsp.dll`` during ``BeforeBuild``-targets to ``$(TargetDir)``
